Welcome To Our IMEX!
IMEX is a medium-sized producer of high-tech toy products, which has its plant in the rural area of Europe. In the past, the company has operated on the European market and it is planning to launch the X and Y product on the US market. In both markets, the demand for educational high-quality toys is expected to increase slightly in the years to come. Neither your company nor your competitors have managed to dominate the European market. The company has been running a selective product strategy for some years now. Consequently, you offer your customers only two types of products, i.e. electronically geared X-games and Y-games. At the top management level, the introduction of a new product line of Z-games is being studied. The market image and position of the products X and Y are very different, although they can be considered equivalent in the field of production and distribution similar raw materials, machines, operators, storage conditions, transportation containers, and distribution channels.
